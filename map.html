how the map works

Update schedule
the community map is reviewed and updated monthly using the newest collector submissions.
Availability status
available: matches are currently available.
not available: no matches available right now.
used to be available: matches were previously available but are no longer confirmed.
why we do this: locations often change in terms of match availability, so even though something might say not available, you might get lucky with matches there!. if you have any updates on locations, please submit them here!
Using the map in Google Maps
after tapping “save to your phone,” the map should open automatically in google maps. to refer to in the future, open the Google Maps app and go to You → scroll down to Maps. Your saved map will appear there.
